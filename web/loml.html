    LOML

    Now, let's delve into something that holds a special place in my heart. But before we dive in, here's a heads-up - I might just talk about K-pop a tad too much, so please bear with me.
    I mentioned earlier that watching KDramas is one of my hobbies, but my love for all things Korean goes beyond that. I'm also a huge fan of K-pop. I understand there are many misconceptions and negative stereotypes surrounding K-pop, especially among non-K-pop fans. Allow me to share what K-pop truly means to me and clear up some of those misconceptions.

    My journey into K-pop began back in grade 7, right around the peak of Twice's and Blackpink's careers. I vividly recall tuning in to their music every day at the local computer shop after school. It was like my daily reward after a tiring day!

    K-pop, to me, is more than just music. It's a significant part of my life. During some of my toughest moments, the music, talent, attitudes, and humor of these K-pop groups have been a lifeline. Their incredible sense of humor never fails to make me smile, laugh, and giggle. It's hard to put into words, but I can confidently say that I find the strength to face each day because of these K-pop groups.

    I'm what they call a "multi-stan," meaning I'm a fan of many groups spanning different K-pop generations. However, there are two groups that have held a special place in my heart from the very beginning - Seventeen and Twice. I can honestly say that they are the loves of my life. Let me introduce you to my ult groups.


    Seventeen is a remarkable boy group made up of 13 members - I know, it sounds a bit puzzling, right? Well, let me break it down for you. In their group, they have 3 sub-units. First, there's the hip-hop unit, consisting of 4 members (Vernon, Scoups, Mingyu, and Wonwoo). Then, there's the dance unit, also with 4 members (Hoshi, Dino, The8, and Jun). Finally, there's the vocal unit, made up of 5 members (Seungkwan, DK, Woozi, Joshua, and Jeonghan). Add these up: 13 members + 3 sub-units + 1 whole group, and you get Seventeen. Makes sense now, right? Seventeen was brought together by Pledis Co. quite a while back. The company scouted these talented individuals and spent several years training them. You can catch glimpses of their journey in the green room through their pre-debut videos on YouTube (if you know, you know). Here's a fun fact: Seventeen actually has 3 leaders - Woozi for the vocal team, Hoshi for the dance team, and Scoups for the hip-hop team and the entire group. My personal journey with Seventeen is relatively recent. While I had heard of them before, I didn't become a full-fledged fan until earlier this year. But here's the thing, as Carats (that's what Seventeen's fans are called) say, "You didn't find them late; you found them exactly when you needed them." That quote gave me chills. I started getting into them through short clips on TikTok that showcased their incredible bond and humor. Eventually, I found myself on their YouTube channel, binge-watching their videos, and losing track of time. They became my ultimate comfort zone. Let me tell you, they are some of the funniest idols I've encountered in my 7 years as a K-pop stan. Sometimes, I forget they're idols; I swear they could moonlight as comedians. But honestly, who's complaining? I'll never get tired of watching these boys goofing around like children. Now, let's talk about who my bias is. Honestly, if you're a Carat, choosing just one bias is like trying to pick your favorite star in the night sky. The members have a way of pulling you in different directions every day, making it a delightful dilemma. But here's the thing, that's what makes Seventeen so special. Each member possesses unique attributes and characteristics that will have you falling head over heels for them. I could go on and on, but I'll stop here. I'll leave some of their discography below, so it's up to you whether you want to dive deeper. However, I can guarantee you that stanning Seventeen will be one of the smartest decisions you'll ever make in your life.

    Twice is a girl group formed through a survival show called "Sixteen," consisting of 9 talented members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. My ultimate bias is Momoring, the dancing machine. I discovered and fell in love with them in 2019 when their iconic songs "Fancy" and "Feel Special" were taking the K-pop world by storm. Besides Blackpink, no other group has reached the same level of importance in my life as Twice. Their "Time to Twice" episodes became my daily dose of happiness, especially during the pandemic when going out was limited. They rescued me from sadness and loneliness, and that's why my loyalty to them runs deep. By the way, did I mention that my bias is Momo? My bad, but I just adore her. She's the clumsiest and funniest, yet she's so mother. Her determination and hard work during their survival show truly resonate with me. This page isn't nearly enough for me to cover everything about Twice, so I'll stop my chatter and let you explore and discover them for yourself. Below, you'll find Twice's discography from their debut to their latest releases. Enjoy, and stan Twice for better life!
